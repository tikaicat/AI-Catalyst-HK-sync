**2. Notion CRM (6 Databases)**

Six Notion databases form the operating system: (1) Subscriber Pipeline --- Newsletter subscriber sources and conversion tracking. (2) Content Calendar --- all 7 channels scheduling. (3) Affiliate Partners --- V5 database mirror with monthly revenue. (4) Newsletter Tracker --- per-issue metrics. (5) Digital Products --- product list, pricing, sales data. (6) Weekly Reviews --- all KPI summaries. See Doc 11 for schemas.
