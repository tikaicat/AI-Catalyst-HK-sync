**Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)**

**Week 1: Infrastructure (Days 1-7)**

Day 1-2: 10Web AI site launch. Register at 10web.io (AI Premium \$15/mo). Use AI builder. Install: RankMath, WPML, ThirstyAffiliates, Google Site Kit, WPForms, WP Chat App.

Day 2-3: Notion CRM + MCP setup. Create 6 databases: Subscriber Pipeline, Content Calendar, Affiliate Partners, Newsletter Tracker, Digital Products, Weekly Reviews. Connect Notion MCP via OAuth.

Day 3: Beehiiv newsletter setup. Register beehiiv.com. Brand: name「AI實戰情報」. Embed signup on every 10Web page. Send first free issue immediately.

Day 4-7: Write and publish first 5 blog posts. Set up all social channels: WhatsApp, FB, IG, LinkedIn, Threads. Join affiliate networks: PartnerStack + Impact.com + Reditus (signup URLs in Doc 05 V5).

**Week 2-3: Content Blitz (Days 8-21)**

Target: 1 blog post per day, reaching 20 posts by Day 21. Each 2,000-3,000 TC characters. Priority: comparison articles (3-5% conversion) \> reviews \> listicles. Every post includes: target keyword, 2-3 affiliate links, FAQ schema, Newsletter signup CTA. Daily Newsletter free edition. Social media: n8n auto-generates 15-20 posts/day (Doc 15), you spend 30 min reviewing.

**Week 4: Automation + Review (Days 22-30)**

Deploy n8n to VPS. Build core workflows: daily social content generation → Google Drive (Doc 15), new subscriber → Notion, welcome email automation, weekly KPI report. Month 1 review: compile data, identify top-performing content types, set Month 2 targets.
